My Fish

Mandarinfish

scientific name:

Pterosynchiropus splendidus

length:

about 3 inches

given name:

Gerry

location:

Malaysia, Indonesia, the Philippines, and Australia

diet:

In the wild & in fish tanks they are very picky eaters. They eat mainly small worms, protozoans, and small crustaceans.

Where My Fish are From

Peninsular Malaysia

Peninsular Malaysia (Malay: Semenanjung Malaysia), also known as Malaya or West Malaysia, is the part of Malaysia which occupies the southern half of the Malay Peninsula and the surrounding islands.
